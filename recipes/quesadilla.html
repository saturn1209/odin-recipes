Quesadilla Recipe


Odin Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 large white tortillas
  • 50 g of mozzarela cheese
  • 1 slice of ham

Steps

  1. Heat a pan up
  2. Add the cheese onto the pan so it starts melting
  3. As the cheese melts, add the ham on top
  4. When the cheese is fully melted add the tortilla on top
  5. Flip over and cover with the second tortilla
  6. Enjoy!
