Scrambled Eggs Recipe


Odin Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 large white eggs
  • 2 ounces of egg yolks
  • 50 g of spanish chorizo

Steps

  1. Heat a pan up
  2. As soon as the pan is hot, add the chorizo directly to the pan
  3. Crack the two eggs and beat together with the egg yolks
  4. As soon as the chorizo starts getting cripsy add the egg and yolk mix
  5. Ensure you stir the mix constantly so it cookes evenly
  6. Enjoy!
