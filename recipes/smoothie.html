Smoothie Recipe


Odin Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 150 g of kefir
  • 90 g of frozen strawberries
  • 30 g of frozen blueberries
  • 5 g of honey amaranth
  • 5 g of walnuts
  • 10 g of shelled pistachios
  • 5 g of chocolate chips
  • 100 ml of milk

Steps

  1. Throw all the ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend for 30 seconds
  3. Enjoy!
